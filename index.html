Dizytle

  • Save the data!!
  • High Concept: Using gravity, get the data (the yellow boxes) to the goal at the bottom. Remember to watch out for yourself (the white ball) while your at it!
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the player to feel frustration or determination.
  • Genre: Arcade puzzle game!
  • Game play: Use the arrow keys to change gravity's direction. Use the WAD keys to move yourself to safety.
  • Prototype goal: Tried to figure out how shift gravity (and did). Tried to figure out how to turn the camera or world view (couldn't figure it out).
  • Features: Gravity and computer inner-workings?
  • Setting/Premise: You (in white) are a motherboard trying to save your data from the virus!
  • Story: Oh no! A virus has attacked your home! You know you wont make it out alive, but what about your data? You can try shifting it through the system to an external hard-drive! But can you do it before the virus consumes you?
  • Target Audience: People who like puzzle games.
  • Play time: A while. It may seem short at first, but trust me, it takes a while!
  • Strategies: THINK BEFORE YOU MOVE! Make sure you (in white) are in a safe place before shifting the gravity!
